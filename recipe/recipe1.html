About Strawberry Custard dish

Ingredients:


  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, chopped
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons custard powder (usually vanilla flavored)
  • 3-4 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Fresh strawberries for garnish (optional)

Instructions:


Step 1: Prepare the Strawberries
->Wash and chop the strawberries into small pieces. Set aside a few for garnishing, if desired. Place the chopped strawberries in a blender and blend until smooth. Set aside the puree.


Step 2: Mix Custard Powder
->In a small bowl, take 2 tablespoons of custard powder and mix with 1/4 cup of milk until smooth, with no lumps.


Step 3: Heat Milk and Sugar
->In a saucepan, heat the remaining 1.75 cups of milk over medium heat. Add the sugar and stir until it dissolves completely.


Step 4: Add Custard Mixture
->Slowly add the custard powder mixture to the heated milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Continue stirring and cook the mixture until it thickens to your desired custard consistency (usually 3â€“5 minutes).


Step 5: Add Strawberry Puree
->Remove the custard from heat and allow it to cool for a minute. Stir in the strawberry puree and mix well. Optionally, add a drop of vanilla extract for extra flavor.


Step 6: Chill and Serve
->Pour the strawberry custard into serving bowls and refrigerate for at least an hour to set. Garnish with fresh strawberries before serving.


Enjoy your creamy, fruity strawberry custard!
