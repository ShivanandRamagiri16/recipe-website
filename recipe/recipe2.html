About Egg Tomato Curry

Ingredients:


  • 4 to 5 boiled eggs
  • 3 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:


Step 1: Boil the Eggs
Boil the eggs for about 8 to 10 minutes. Cool, peel, and set aside. Optional: Lightly fry the boiled eggs in oil until golden for extra flavor.


Step 2: Prepare the Masala Base
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté the onions until golden brown, then add the ginger-garlic paste. Cook for another 1 to 2 minutes until the raw smell of ginger-garlic disappears.


Step 3: Add Tomatoes and Spices
Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Stir and cook the spices with the tomatoes until oil separates from the masala (3–4 minutes).


Step 4: Add Water and Simmer
Add about 1/2 cup of water to make a curry sauce. Adjust water for your desired consistency. Simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.


Step 5: Add Eggs and Season
Add the boiled eggs to the curry, gently stirring to coat them with the masala. Sprinkle garam masala and adjust salt to taste. Simmer for another 2–3 minutes to let the eggs absorb the flavors.


Step 6: Garnish and Serve
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.


Enjoy your delicious Tomato Egg Curry!
