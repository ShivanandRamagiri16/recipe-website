About Chicken Kabab

Ingredients:


  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Skewers (if using wooden skewers, soak in water for 30 minutes)

Instructions:


Step 1: Prepare the Marinade
In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, salt, and oil. Mix well to form a smooth marinade.


Step 2: Marinate the Chicken
Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1–2 hours (or up to overnight for maximum flavor).


Step 3: Prepare the Skewers
If using skewers, thread the marinated chicken pieces onto each skewer, leaving a little space between each piece for even cooking.


Step 4: Cook the Chicken Kababs
Grilling: Preheat the grill and cook the skewered chicken for about 10–15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Oven-Baking: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Place the skewers on a baking tray and bake for 20–25 minutes, turning halfway. Pan-Frying: Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat. Place the chicken pieces (without skewers) in the pan and cook until golden brown on all sides.


Step 5: Serve
Serve hot with a sprinkle of lemon juice, freshly chopped cilantro, and onion slices. Accompany with mint chutney or your favorite dipping sauce.


Enjoy your flavorful and juicy chicken kababs!
