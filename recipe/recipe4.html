About Chicken Kabab

Ingredients:


  • 500g fish fillets (e.g., tilapia, cod, or salmon)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour or cornflour (for extra crispiness, optional)
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges and chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:


Step 1: Prepare the Fish
Rinse the fish fillets and pat them dry with a paper towel. This will help the marinade stick better.


Step 2: Marinate the Fish
In a bowl, combine turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, lemon juice, and salt. Coat the fish fillets with this spice mixture, making sure each piece is evenly covered. Optionally, sprinkle rice flour or cornflour over the fish for an extra-crispy texture. Let the fish marinate for 15–30 minutes to absorb the flavors.


Step 3: Heat the Oil
Heat oil in a skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Make sure there’s enough oil to shallow-fry the fish.


Step 4: Fry the Fish
Carefully place the marinated fish fillets in the hot oil. Fry for about 3–4 minutes on each side, or until the fish is golden brown and crispy. Avoid overcrowding the pan; fry in batches if needed.


Step 5: Drain and Serve
Remove the fried fish and place on a paper towel to drain any excess oil. Garnish with fresh lemon wedges and chopped coriander leaves.


Enjoy your crispy, flavorful fish fry! Serve it with rice, salad, or chutney.
